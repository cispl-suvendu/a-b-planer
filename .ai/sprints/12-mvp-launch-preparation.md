# Sprint 12 — MVP Launch Preparation

## Sprint Goal

Prepare Landing Page A/B Planner for real users by completing product polish, deployment readiness, analytics, and beta launch processes.

---

# Sprint Objectives

By the end of this sprint:

- Production environment is ready.
- Marketing landing page is complete.
- User onboarding works.
- Analytics tracking exists.
- Beta users can use the product.
- Launch checklist is completed.
