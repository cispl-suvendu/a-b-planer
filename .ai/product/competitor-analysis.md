# Competitor Analysis

## Purpose

This document defines the competitive landscape for Landing Page A/B Planner.

It helps product, design, engineering, and marketing teams understand:

- Existing solutions
- Market expectations
- Competitive gaps
- Product differentiation

The goal is not to copy competitors.

The goal is to build a product users choose because it solves the problem better.

---

# Market Category

Landing Page A/B Planner operates in the intersection of:

- Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)
- AI Website Analysis
- Growth Marketing Tools
- Experimentation Platforms
- AI Productivity Software

# Competitor Analysis

---

# Competitor: Optimizely

## Category

Enterprise experimentation platform.

## Strengths

- Powerful experimentation engine
- Enterprise adoption
- Advanced targeting
- Statistical testing

## Weaknesses

- Expensive
- Complex setup
- Requires existing experimentation knowledge

## Opportunity

Provide experimentation strategy before implementation.

---

# Competitor: VWO

## Category

CRO testing platform.

## Strengths

- A/B testing
- Heatmaps
- Analytics
- Enterprise features

## Weaknesses

- Focuses on running tests
- Users still need ideas
- Complex for small teams

## Opportunity

Help users decide what to test first.

---

# Competitor: Hotjar

## Category

Behavior analytics.

## Strengths

- Heatmaps
- Session recordings
- User feedback

## Weaknesses

- Shows behavior
- Does not create prioritized experiments

## Opportunity

Convert observations into actionable experiments.

---

# Competitor: Google Analytics

## Category

Analytics platform.

## Strengths

- Free
- Widely adopted
- Powerful reporting

## Weaknesses

- Requires expertise
- Does not recommend actions

## Opportunity

Turn data into decisions.

---

# Competitor: ChatGPT / General AI

## Category

AI assistant.

## Strengths

- Flexible
- Fast
- Accessible

## Weaknesses

- Generic output
- No specialized workflow
- No prioritization framework
- No structured CRO reports

## Opportunity

Build a specialized AI CRO workflow.

---

# Market Gap

Current solutions require users to answer:

"What does this data mean?"

"What should we test?"

"What should we prioritize?"

Landing Page A/B Planner answers:

"What experiment should I run next?"

---

# Our Differentiation

## 1. AI CRO Specialist

We are not a general AI assistant.

We specialize in conversion optimization.

---

## 2. Prioritized Recommendations

Competitors often provide:

Lists.

We provide:

Ranked experiments.

---

## 3. Complete Experiment Format

Every recommendation includes:

- Problem
- Hypothesis
- Variant A
- Variant B
- Impact
- Confidence
- Difficulty
- Implementation
- Success metric

---

## 4. Founder-Friendly

Designed for users without CRO expertise.

---

## 5. Affordable

Positioned between:

Free AI tools

and

Expensive CRO consultants.

---

# Why Users Choose Us

Users choose Landing Page A/B Planner because:

- They don't know what to test.
- They need expert-level guidance.
- They want fast answers.
- They need prioritized actions.
- They cannot afford CRO consultants.

---

# Competitive Risks

## Risk: AI commoditization

General AI models may improve.

Response:

Build specialized workflows, data structures, and user experience.

---

## Risk: Large CRO platforms add AI

Response:

Focus on simplicity and speed for startups.

---

## Risk: Low-quality AI recommendations

Response:

Improve prompts, evaluation, and structured outputs.

---

# Competitive Strategy

We win by being:

- Simpler
- Faster
- More actionable
- More affordable
- More focused

---

# Product Implications

The product should avoid:

- Enterprise complexity
- Too many settings
- Generic AI chat interfaces
- Overwhelming dashboards

The product should emphasize:

- Clear recommendations
- Professional reports
- Fast results
- Beautiful UX

---

# AI Guidance

When suggesting features:

Consider:

1. Does this improve our differentiation?
2. Does this make recommendations better?
3. Does this increase user trust?
4. Does this make us easier to use?

Avoid features that simply copy competitors.
