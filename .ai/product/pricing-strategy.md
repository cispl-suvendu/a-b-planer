# Pricing Strategy

## Purpose

This document defines the pricing philosophy, packaging strategy, upgrade experience, and future pricing evolution for Landing Page A/B Planner.

The goal is to create a pricing model that:

- Provides immediate value.
- Encourages product adoption.
- Converts satisfied users into paying customers.
- Maintains customer trust.
- Supports long-term SaaS growth.

---

# Pricing Philosophy

Our pricing follows one core principle:

> Users should experience meaningful value before paying.

We believe the best conversion strategy is not aggressive selling.

The product should naturally demonstrate its value through high-quality AI analysis and actionable recommendations.

---

# Current Pricing Model

## Freemium + Subscription

The MVP uses:

Free Plan

↓

Experience product value

↓

Reach usage limit

↓

Upgrade to Pro

---

# Free Plan Strategy

## Purpose

The free plan exists to:

- Reduce signup friction.
- Allow users to experience the product.
- Build trust.
- Create product habit.
- Demonstrate AI quality.

---

## Free Plan Limits

Users receive:

4 lifetime analyses

This limit is intentionally:

- Large enough to experience value.
- Small enough to encourage upgrades.

---

## Free User Experience

Free users can:

- Create account
- Analyze pages
- View reports
- Access history
- Understand product value

Free users should never feel like they are using a restricted demo.

---

# Pro Plan Strategy

## Current Price

$19/month

---

## Target Customer

Best suited for:

- Startup founders
- Growth marketers
- Product managers
- Freelancers
- Small agencies

---

## Core Value

Pro users pay for:

- Unlimited experimentation ideas.
- Continuous optimization.
- Time savings.
- Professional CRO guidance.

---

# Pricing Psychology

The pricing should communicate:

"An affordable AI CRO assistant."

Not:

"An expensive enterprise optimization platform."

The product should feel accessible to early-stage teams.

---

# Upgrade Trigger Strategy

The strongest upgrade moment is:

After users receive value.

Examples:

## Trigger 1

User completes multiple analyses.

Message:

"You have used your free analyses. Continue optimizing unlimited pages with Pro."

---

## Trigger 2

User starts a new analysis after reaching limit.

Show:

- Value reminder
- Previous reports
- Pro benefits

---

## Trigger 3

User views premium-related features.

Example:

Future PDF export

Message:

"Upgrade to Pro to unlock advanced optimization workflows."

---

# Upgrade Experience Rules

Upgrade prompts must:

Explain value.

Show benefits.

Avoid pressure.

Avoid artificial urgency.

Never:

- Block existing reports.
- Hide previous work.
- Use misleading messages.

# Pricing Rules

The following rules are mandatory:

- Free users receive exactly four lifetime analyses.
- Usage limits are enforced server-side.
- Frontend cannot decide subscription permissions.
- Subscription status comes from backend.
- Stripe is the source of billing truth.
- Existing reports are always preserved.
- Pricing changes require updating this document.
