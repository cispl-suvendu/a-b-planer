# Value Proposition

## Purpose

This document defines why customers should choose Landing Page A/B Planner over alternative solutions.

It establishes our unique positioning, customer promise, competitive advantages, and messaging principles.

Every feature, marketing campaign, onboarding experience, and AI recommendation should reinforce this value proposition.

---

# Positioning Statement

Landing Page A/B Planner is an AI-powered experimentation planning platform that helps founders, marketers, and product teams discover, prioritize, and implement high-impact A/B tests without requiring deep Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) expertise.

Unlike generic AI assistants or traditional A/B testing tools, our platform delivers structured, explainable, and prioritized experimentation roadmaps that teams can act on immediately.

---

# Customer Promise

We promise every customer that they will:

- Know what to test next.
- Understand why it matters.
- Receive recommendations they can implement immediately.
- Save hours of manual analysis.
- Make more confident optimization decisions.

The product should remove uncertainty, not create it.

---

# Core Value Proposition

Users don't pay us to analyze HTML.

Users don't pay us to generate AI text.

Users pay us for:

**Clarity.**

They want to know:

> "What should I test first to improve conversions?"

Everything in the product should help answer that question.

---

# Our Competitive Advantages

## Structured Intelligence

We don't produce a wall of AI-generated text.

We produce:

- Executive summaries
- Prioritized hypotheses
- Variant ideas
- Implementation guidance
- Success metrics
- Clear reasoning

The output resembles a professional CRO consulting report.

---

## Prioritization

Most competitors generate lists.

We generate ranked recommendations using a transparent formula:

Priority Score = Impact × Confidence ÷ Difficulty

Users immediately know where to begin.

---

## Explainable AI

Every recommendation includes:

- Problem
- Evidence
- Hypothesis
- Expected outcome
- Confidence
- Difficulty
- Business reasoning

Trust is created through transparency.

---

## Business-Focused

Our recommendations focus on:

- Revenue
- Conversion
- Lead generation
- User behavior
- Business outcomes

Not technical perfection.

---

## Minimal Learning Curve

The platform is designed for people who are not CRO experts.

Users should be productive within minutes.

# Product Decision Filter

Every new feature should strengthen at least one of these differentiators:

- Better prioritization
- Better recommendations
- Better explainability
- Faster workflows
- Greater trust
- Simpler UX

If it doesn't strengthen our value proposition, reconsider its priority.

---

# AI Guidance

When generating reports, AI should:

- Recommend, not overwhelm.
- Explain, not just describe.
- Prioritize, not list.
- Focus on business impact.
- Deliver consulting-quality insights.

Every report should answer:

> "If I only implement one recommendation this week, what should it be?"
