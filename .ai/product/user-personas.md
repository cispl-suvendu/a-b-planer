# User Personas

## Purpose

This document defines the primary user personas for the Landing Page A/B Planner.

Unlike the broader market segments in `target-audience.md`, personas represent realistic users with goals, frustrations, workflows, and success criteria.

Every product decision should improve the experience for one or more personas.

---

# Persona 1 — Startup Founder

## Profile

**Name:** Alex Carter  
**Role:** Founder & CEO  
**Company Size:** 2–15 employees  
**Industry:** SaaS / AI Startup  
**Experience:** Non-technical to moderately technical

---

### Goals

- Acquire more customers
- Increase landing page conversions
- Validate product messaging
- Grow without hiring expensive consultants

---

### Frustrations

- Doesn't know what to test
- Limited marketing budget
- No CRO expertise
- Too many opinions, not enough data
- Little time for optimization

---

### Daily Workflow

- Review analytics
- Launch marketing campaigns
- Talk to customers
- Update landing pages
- Measure growth

---

### Success Looks Like

- Higher conversion rates
- Faster decision-making
- Clear optimization roadmap

---

### Product Usage

Usually performs:

- 1–3 analyses per week
- Reviews recommendations personally
- Shares implementation tasks with the team

---

### Technical Skill

★★★★☆

Comfortable with modern SaaS tools but not a developer.

# Shared User Needs

All personas want:

- Clear recommendations
- Professional reports
- High confidence
- Minimal setup
- Fast analysis
- Actionable next steps
- Transparent reasoning

---

# Shared User Fears

Users worry that:

- AI recommendations are generic
- AI misunderstands their business
- Reports are difficult to trust
- Recommendations are hard to implement

The product should continuously reduce these concerns through explainability and evidence.

---

# UX Implications

The interface should:

- Require minimal onboarding
- Explain progress during analysis
- Highlight quick wins first
- Use business-friendly language
- Minimize cognitive load
- Avoid unnecessary settings

# AI Guidance

When generating recommendations, AI should assume the user:

- Has limited time
- Wants practical advice
- Prefers prioritized actions
- Appreciates measurable outcomes
- Values clarity over technical detail

Recommendations should read like advice from an experienced CRO consultant rather than an academic report.
